Another example of rock art which -like the monumental petroglyphs of Amazonas State- was doubtless intended to be visible from far away, is the Geoglyph (figure drawn on the ground). 

The geoglyphs of Nazca, Perú are known throughout the whole world, but very few Venezuelans and fewer foreigners know of the existence of the geoglyph in the Carabobo state or of the enigmatic tales that place them also in the Yaracuy state. 

The ANAR documents them in videos and photographs taken from the air, maps and illustrated publications. of Nazca
