    Among the activities that ANAR develops under its Program Education and dissemination of knowledge Venezuelan rock art heritage, as a teaching strategy conservation; is the Project: DEMONSTRATIONS VENEZUELAN CAVE AND THE SCHOOL, developed so inter and transdisciplinary, through planning topic different areas of learning, secondary and subsystems primary.

    The project aims to transfer knowledge and experience dissemination and promotion of the Venezuelan Rupestrian Manifestations in Basic Education, Media and diversified; offering through the development of different workshops and teaching materials structured levels, aimed at teachers and students, content validated from the point of view scientific, cultural, social and curricular; aiming essential that involved as subject in Projects Teaching the Ministry of Education and the official curriculum at all levels from kindergarten through fifth grade in high school.
